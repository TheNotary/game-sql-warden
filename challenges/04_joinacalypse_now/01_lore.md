# Joinocalypse Now — The Ragged Oracle Speaks

A flickering fluorescent light crackles above you as you step into the next cubicle of the endless dungeon.  
The gray-carpeted floor is stained with the ghosts of long-forgotten stand-up meetings.

From behind a leaning stack of obsolete HR policy binders, an old man emerges.

His beard is frayed, wiry, and far too animated to be natural.  
His robe is actually a repurposed corporate bathrobe, and his oak staff, though dull, occasionally sparks with what you hope is magic and not static electricity.

“Traveler!” he croaks, jabbing the staff dangerously close to your face.

“You stand at the precipice of *the Joinocalypse*, a terror forged in the break-rooms of yore!  
Many interns have perished attempting the **Multi-Way Join**. Some… never found their way out of the query planner.”

He slams the staff onto the ground. A printer in the corner immediately errors with a paper jam.

“You must create a VIEW—yes, yes, a shimmering construct of truth and UNION—named **solution**. Only by solving the puzzle of tables intertwined may you pass deeper into the labyrinth.”

He leans close and whispers:

“Trust not the LEFT JOIN!  
Fear not the INNER JOIN!  
And may your ON clauses never be NULL…”

He steps back dramatically.

“Now go!  
Face the Joinocalypse!  
And pray your execution time stays low…”
