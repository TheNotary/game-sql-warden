*The dim hum of fluorescent crystals echoes through the cubical corridors. A ragged old man, beard like frayed ethernet cords, hobbles forward on a dull oak staff that sparks occasionally with outdated magic. He squints at you, grinning with too many teeth and too much enthusiasm.*

"Ahh! A fresh traveler, lost in this mystic-corporate maze of quarterly despair! Welcome, welcome, brave soul!" he cackles, tapping his staff against a cube-wall. The wall flickers with spreadsheets.

"In this dungeon, nothing is simple—especially numbers. Most who wander these halls fall victim to *pivot fiends* and *join wraiths*. But **you**..." He pokes your chest. "You might just survive."

"Before you lies a challenge of terrifying complexity, forged in the great HR furnaces of the top fang-forges. I call it—" His voice booms and echoes unnaturally: **"Group By or Die Tryin'!"**

"Somewhere in this database lurk records—datasets twisted and tangled! Your task is to conjure a VIEW, yes, a mighty VIEW named `solution`! Only through grouping, and grouping-upon-grouping, and those strange arcane structures they call *grouping sets*, can you illuminate the hidden truths within. But beware! Many analysts enter these depths confident, only to be devoured by their own aggregations!"

He leans closer, whispering: "Create the VIEW, traveler. Let your query reveal every level the data longs to whisper. Then—*and only then*—may you pass through the cubicle gates unharmed."

"Now go! The spreadsheets hunger!"
