Deep within the cubical dungeon, where every wall is a perfect right angle and every shadow falls in straight lines, a lone figure hunches over a glowing terminal. His cloak is woven from deprecated schemas; his eyes flicker like cursors waiting for input.

"Ah... a new traveler," he rasps, voice echoing across concrete facets. "You seek the Query of Ascension, do you not? Very well. In these blocks of stone and logic, only those who bend data to their will may proceed."

He leans closer. "Before you lies a puzzle of ranking... of selection... of choosing the worthy few from the many. Only the sharpest mind can extract the top records from each faction. Fail, and your query shall return naught but shame. Succeed... and the dungeon may just reveal its next chamber."

"Now go, traveler. Show me how you choose the best of each tribe."
